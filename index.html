Option 1: Vertical Timeline

Submission Guide

Please follow these steps carefully to ensure your manuscript is ready for review by our editorial board.

1

Confirm fit

Your submission should directly relate to female-headed households, displaced single mothers, or the gendered impacts of displacement (refugees, asylum seekers, or IDPs). We also welcome pieces that connect evidence to practical program or policy recommendations.

2

Choose your submission type and length

Use these ranges as guidance (we can be flexible for the first issue):

  • Original Research: 4,000-8,000 words
  • Review Articles: 4,000-8,000 words
  • Commentaries / Letters: 800-1,500 words
  • Book Reviews: 800-1,500 words
  • Editorial / Opinion: 1,200-2,500 words
3

Prepare your files

Please submit:

  1. Manuscript file (Word .docx preferred; PDF acceptable)
  2. Title page with author details (name, affiliation, email, short bio, ORCID if you have one)
  3. Abstract (150-250 words) + 5-7 keywords
  4. Tables/figures embedded in the manuscript and also attached separately if possible (PNG/JPG for images)

File Naming Convention

JournalSubmission_LastName_FirstName_Type_Date

Example:

JournalSubmission_Riggs_Kristi_OriginalResearch_2026-09-30

4

Ethics and protection check

If your work involves human participants or sensitive contexts, include a short statement describing:

  • Consent approach and privacy protections (including de-identification)
  • Any IRB/ethics review (if applicable)
  • How you minimized risk to participants and communities

If you cannot share certain details publicly, tell us what you can provide and why.

5

Citations and formatting

  • Use APA (7th edition) unless another style is essential to your discipline (tell us if so).
  • Include full citations and DOIs/URLs where available.
  • Use clear headings and keep formatting simple (no heavy styling needed).
6

Email your submission

Send your files to: contact@therefugeearchive.org

Required Subject Line

Journal Submission_NAME

Example:

Journal Submission_Amina Yusuf

In your email body, include:

  • Submission type
  • Title of the piece
  • A 1-2 sentence summary
  • Confirmation you have the right to submit this work and that it is not under review elsewhere (or clearly state if it is)
